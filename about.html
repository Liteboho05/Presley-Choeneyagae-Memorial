His Story

Presley Chweneyagae was born in 1984 in South Africa. He rose to international prominence for his starring role in the Oscar-winning film Tsotsi (2005), where he portrayed a young gangster on a path of redemption.

Beyond acting, Presley was a voice for the youth, deeply involved in theatre and community initiatives. His powerful performances and dedication to the arts made him a beloved figure in South African culture.
