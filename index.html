Welcome

Welcome to the official tribute website for Presley Chweneyagae, a beloved South African actor, humanitarian, and storyteller. Presley captured the hearts of millions through his iconic role in Tsotsi and his lifelong commitment to uplifting communities through art.

Here we celebrate his life, his legacy, and the unforgettable impact he had on the world.

Message of Remembrance

Presley passed away on 27 May 2025 due to respiratory complications at the age of 40. Though he is no longer with us, his voice lives on through every scene he graced, every life he touched, and every story he helped tell. May his soul rest in eternal peace.
