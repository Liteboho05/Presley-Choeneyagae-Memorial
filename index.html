Welcome

This digital tombstone honors the life and legacy of Presley Chweneyagae, whose performance in Tsotsi left a lasting impact on South African and global cinema.

About Presley

Born in 1984, Presley Chweneyagae was an actor known for his deep emotional roles and voice in youth empowerment. His breakthrough role in Tsotsi made him a national treasure.

Achievements

  • Oscar-winning role in Tsotsi
  • Celebrated actor in film and theatre
  • Community leader and mentor
  • National awards and recognition
