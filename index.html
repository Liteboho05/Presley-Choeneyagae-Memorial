Welcome

This digital tombstone is created in memory of Presley Chweneyagae, whose legacy in film, theatre, and culture continues to inspire us all.

About Presley

Presley was a South African actor best known for his powerful role in the film Tsotsi, which won an Academy Award. Born in 1984, he became a voice of hope for many young South Africans.

Achievements

  • Starred in Tsotsi (2005) – Oscar Winner
  • Award-winning actor in South African theatre
  • Advocate for youth and community arts
  • TV appearances and local film industry leader
